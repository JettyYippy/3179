Countries' Olympic's Performance

The Olympics, also known as the Olympic Games, are an international sporting competition that alternates between the Summer and Winter Games every four years. Athletes from all around the world participate in a variety of sports and disciplines at these competitions. The ancient Greek Olympic Games served as the model for the contemporary Olympic Games, which were revived by Pierre de Coubertin in the late 19th century. The Games showcase the best athletes in the world while encouraging global cooperation, sportsmanship, and fair competition.
