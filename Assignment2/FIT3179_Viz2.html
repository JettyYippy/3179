Countries' Olympic's Performance

The Summer Olympics is a global multi-sport event held every four years, featuring athletes from around the world in a wide range of summer sports. It is organized by the International Olympic Committee and encourages cooperation, good sportsmanship, and athletic excellence. The Games have a long history that dates back to the time of ancient Greece, and they now serve as an inspiration for international collaboration and competitiveness.
